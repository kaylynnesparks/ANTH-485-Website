Parks that protect Garry Oak Ecosystems in Victoria.

Parks that are currently protecting Garry Oak ecosytems in Victoria are highlighted in red.

It is very obvious from this map how small and fragmented Garry Oak ecosystems are. Less than 5% of Garry Oak ecosystems remain in good condition.

What are the values of the parks that protect these ecosystems?

Values of Parks expressed through a Word Cloud.

Voyant analyzes the values of the parks expressed through a word cloud. The words used were extracted from the park websites.

Final Thoughts.

According to the word cloud, parks are more concerned about community, recreation, and the region more than Garry Oak ecosystems. Considering how few Garry Oak ecosystems there are and how fragmented they've become, I believe that focus and effort can be placed on protecting these ecosystems.

There are, however, plenty of organizations dedicated to the survival of Garry Oak ecosystems. The Garry Oak Ecosystem Recovery Team (GOERT), for example, is a well developed organization that works in these parks, among other places, to help restore Garry Oak Ecosystems.
